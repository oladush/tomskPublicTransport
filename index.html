Tomsk public transport

a Bus

The main mode of public transport in the Tomsk city is the bus. In whatever area of​the city you are, the bus stop will always be nearby and you will have time to return home on time. Most importantly, the bus ride won't ruin you. It`s cost is 22 rub.

a Tram

If you have not traveled by tram, you missed a lot. Correct the situation quickly and buy a ticket for the Classic Tomsk Tram. It`s cost is 22 rub but impressions are priceless.
